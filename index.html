Biography

Jim Morrison, original name James Douglas Morrison, was born in December 8th 1943, in Melbourne Florida-U.S. and died in July 3rd 1971, in Paris-France). He was an American singer and songwriter who was the charismatic front man of the psychedelic rock group the Doors.
